Welcome to UniConvo

UniConvo is a scenario-specific digital assistant that offers unprecedented adaptability, dynamic interaction, and engaging animations. Powered by the most advanced scene-specific large language model, it provides high-quality, real-time responses, delivering personalized engagement and revolutionary interactive experiences.

Key Features

  • Personalized Interaction: Enhance user engagement with customizable visual, voice, and personality traits through vivid animations and interactive characters.
  • Rich Customization Strategies: Design specific product details to increase user retention, reduce labor costs, and enhance user experience while generating additional advertising revenue.
  • Scene-Specific Large Language Model: Utilize an advanced framework to enhance response quality, offering precise and context-aware real-time responses.

Application Scenarios

  • Dish Cooking Timer & Reminder: Automatically set a timer for dishes requiring specific cooking times and remind customers to retrieve their food at the optimal time for the best taste.
  • Personalized Seasoning Recommendations: Offer personalized seasoning recipes based on user preferences and guide them to the self-service seasoning station, recording their feedback.
  • Real-Time Service Requests & Emergency Calls: Enable customers to request services anytime, optimizing response time and providing emergency assistance when needed.
  • Activity Introduction & Advertising Promotion: Inform customers about current promotions, new product recommendations, and partner advertisements, enhancing engagement with interactive features like table-side song requests.
